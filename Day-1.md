##  Objective

To understand the roadmap of the **1-Month AI Training Program** and gain an overview of the technologies, tools, and concepts that will be covered throughout the training.

## 📖 Learning Outcome

The first day focused on understanding the complete roadmap of the AI Training Program. We were introduced to the core concepts of modern Artificial Intelligence, emerging AI technologies, industry-relevant engineering roles, and the tools that will be used throughout the training. This session provided a strong conceptual foundation before moving on to practical, hands-on implementation.

## 💡 Key Takeaways

* Understood the structure and goals of the one-month AI training program.
* Learned about the evolution of modern AI and Generative AI.
* Explored important concepts such as LLMs, RAG, Agentic AI, MCP, and Prompt Engineering.
* Gained awareness of AI development workflows and emerging career opportunities.
* Became familiar with popular AI tools and platforms that will be used during the training.

## 🚀 Summary

Day 1 served as an orientation session, introducing the roadmap, essential AI concepts, development methodologies, and industry-standard tools. It laid a solid foundation for the upcoming hands-on sessions and provided a clear understanding of what to expect throughout the training journey.
